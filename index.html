About District

District is a location-based security solution that minimizes risk to your data and your organization by enabling you to implement security measures at a centralized, not end-user, level. The result is uniform, consistent protection for your system security throughout your organization. District helps your mobile workforce safely get their work done wherever they need to be.

District helped me feel so much more secure about traveling with my government issued device.

John Doe from Chicago

Features

  • Multilayer encryption to protect sensitive data
  • Anti-tampering measures to prevent physical hacking
  • Manage access to multiple operating systems on one device, including permissions for specific hardware and software
  • Track assets within the district using integrated RFID technology, including alerts customized for multiple layers of security personnel and types of assets
